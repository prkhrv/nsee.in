About Us

National Scholarship Entrance Examinations is a national level scholarship program provided by an organization which is registered under the Ministry of finance act 1994 is being registered with the central excise department of India.

All India Scholarship Exam is an eligibility test for Engineering Scholarship, Medical Scholarship and Higher Studies for College Students.NSEE are bridging the gap between ambition and knowledge. The NSEE will be conducted all across India and the examination will be conducted in Online mode (Home Based Test & Computer Based Test). The idea to NSEE Scholarship Exam in India is to provide the best education to the talented student by funding to their higher education.

All those students whose families have financial problem and can’t able to afford such a big amount for the higher education can take up All India Scholarship Examination.Read More
